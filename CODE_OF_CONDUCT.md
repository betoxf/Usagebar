# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Usagebar welcoming and respectful for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing the technical substance directly.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and supporting its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or deliberately inflammatory conduct.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or account data without permission.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project. Maintainers may edit or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior is harmful or disruptive.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed fairly and with discretion.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
